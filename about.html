About KCG

Ryan Keating

As president and founder of Keating Consulting Group (KCG), Ryan Keating has advised and worked with more than 80 early stage venture capital seeking and venture backed companies, several as the interim-CFO. Ryan possesses an extensive financial and management consulting background from more than 15 years with venture backed companies and large technology and management consulting firms including Microsoft, Salomon Smith Barney and PricewaterhouseCoopers. Ryan attended Boston University, School of Management and holds a degree in Finance and Information Systems.

KCG Operations Team

Resources are a key component to a company's success. Having access to operational expertise to help establish and support the company is critical. KCG's operations team consist of CPAs, controllers, bookkeepers, HR experts, and financial analysts that can help any organization, especially early stage companies, in vital operations areas such as: all aspects of accounting, creation of financial projections, human resources and benefit management, facilities, executive level administration, and the development of corporate policies and procedures. These essentials can bring the entrepreneur's ideas off the paper, help maintain a functioning work environment, and assist with the growth of an organization.

Keating Consulting has provided the expertise and support for Aria Systems to manage hyper growth in a very dynamic market. Ryan and his team work closely with our executives from business planning through execution, measurement, and Board review.
