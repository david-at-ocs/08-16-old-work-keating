Keating Consulting Group

Accounting Services and Operational Support

Keating Consulting Group (KCG) is a Silicon Valley based financial services firm providing financial and operational leadership and back office support to technology start-ups and early-stage companies.

KCG fuses together its expertise and resources to establish, implement and guide the essential function areas necessary to both venture-capital seeking and venture-backed organizations.

Our services are all designed to assist in business direction, compliance and growth.

KCG Interim CFO Services

KCG managing partner Ryan Keating drives and delivers the financial modeling, value analysis and due diligence framework for the purposes of funding and/or acquisition of early to mid-stage start-ups. KCG's value comes from its expertise in supplying business critical data and analysis that venture capitalists, the Board and executive staff require to understand and make strategic financial decisions.

KCG Accounting and Controller Services

The KCG accounting team develops and manages the many disciplines associated with building and maintaining daily financial activities including AR/AP, audits, planning and purchasing, compliance and revenue recognition. Beyond the necessary support and maintenance, KCG also implements bookkeeping practices, accounting systems, payroll services, tax preparation, policies and procedures, as well as supervision and training of existing staff—all important elements in establishing a complete accounting team.

KCG Operations - Client Services and Human Resources

KCG Operations focuses on or off site administrative assistance and facilities-related functions. KCG is skilled in general office set-up, organization and management. Administratively, KCG has the resources to support executive staff with schedule management, travel, logistics and day-to-day structure.

KCG provides a comprehensive range of HR support services including selection and management of employee benefits, stock administration, payroll, new hire processing, reduction in workforce/termination and the development and implementation of company policies and procedures.
